Site Purpose

This site's purpose is to create awareness and interest patrons of the fiber arts of any experience level and translate those visits into sales. In addition, the site will offer inspiration with displaying a few projects using their yarn.

Scenarios

  • How much yarn is in a skein of yarn.
  • What can I make with this yarn?
  • What weight of yarn is the best to use to make a sweater?
  • How much yarn do I need to make an afghan?
