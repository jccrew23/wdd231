About Me
Welcome to Knotty Intentions! I’m thrilled to share my passion for all things yarn with you! My name is [Your Name], and I opened Knotty Intentions with a vision to create a cozy haven for crafters of all levels. From the moment you step inside, my goal is to inspire your creativity and foster a sense of community among fellow yarn lovers. My journey began years ago when I discovered the joy of knitting and crocheting. The thrill of turning simple yarn into beautiful, handmade pieces captivated me, and I wanted to share that experience with others. Knotty Intentions is a reflection of that passion, filled with a carefully curated selection of high-quality yarns in every color and texture you can imagine. Here, you’ll find everything from soft merinos and luxurious alpacas to vibrant cottons and eco-friendly options. I believe that the right yarn can transform any project, and I’m dedicated to helping you find the perfect materials for your creative vision. But Knotty Intentions isn’t just about the yarn. It’s a place where ideas flourish and friendships blossom. I invite you to join our crafting community, where you can participate in workshops, knitting circles, and events that celebrate creativity and connection. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or just starting out, there’s a space for you here. Thank you for visiting Knotty Intentions! I can’t wait to see the amazing projects you create and to share in this wonderful journey of crafting together.
Happy stitching!
Jalielle Curtis - Owner, Knotty Intentions
